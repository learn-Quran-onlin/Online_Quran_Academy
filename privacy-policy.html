Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Dar Arqam collects, uses, protects, and handles your personal information.

1. Introduction

At Dar Arqam, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal information remains secure. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our website and services.

By using our website and services, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect different types of information, including:

  • Personal Information such as your name, email address, phone number, and country.
  • Student information submitted through registration or trial forms.
  • Usage data including pages visited, browser type, IP address, and device information.
  • Cookies and analytics data used to improve website functionality and user experience.

3. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect may be used to:

  • Provide Quran, Arabic, and Islamic Studies services.
  • Schedule and manage classes and free trial sessions.
  • Communicate with students and parents.
  • Improve our website and educational services.
  • Respond to inquiries and support requests.
  • Ensure security and prevent unauthorized access.

4. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Our website may use cookies and similar technologies to improve user experience and website performance.

  • Remembering user preferences.
  • Analyzing website traffic and visitor behavior.
  • Improving functionality and performance.

You can disable cookies through your browser settings if you prefer.

5. Sharing of Information

Dar Arqam does not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

We may share information only when necessary:

  • To comply with legal obligations.
  • To protect our rights, students, and staff.
  • With trusted service providers helping operate our services.

6. Data Protection & Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse.

  • Secure hosting and encrypted connections (HTTPS).
  • Restricted access to personal information.
  • Regular monitoring and security updates.

7. Lesson Recordings

Some online lessons may be recorded for quality assurance, training, and supervision purposes.

  • Recordings are securely stored.
  • Only authorized staff may access recordings.
  • Recordings are never shared publicly without written consent.

8. Children's Privacy

Protecting children's privacy is extremely important to us. Parents or guardians are responsible for providing consent for students under the age required by applicable laws.

9. Third-Party Services

Our website may use third-party services such as:

  • Google Analytics
  • Zoom or online classroom platforms
  • Payment gateways such as PayPal

These services may collect limited technical information according to their own privacy policies.

10. Your Rights

You may request to:

  • Access your personal information.
  • Correct inaccurate information.
  • Delete your data where legally applicable.
  • Withdraw consent for communications.

11. Changes to This Policy

Dar Arqam reserves the right to update or modify this Privacy Policy at any time. Updates will be posted on this page with the revised date.

12. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • Email: support@dararqam.com
  • Website: https://www.dararqam.com
